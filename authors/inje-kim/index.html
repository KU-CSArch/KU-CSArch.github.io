Inje Kim

Master Student

Korea University

Inje Kim is a graduate student pursuing a master’s degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Korea University. His research interest is in accelerator architecture, GPGPU, graph neural networks (GNNs). His current research focuses on characterizing graph neural networks on GPGPU and acceleration of graph neural networks.

Inje earned his B.S. degree in Electronic & Electrical Engineering at Hongik University. Before entering Korea University, he worked as a undergraduate researcher for two years at Hongik University.
